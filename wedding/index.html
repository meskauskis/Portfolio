Our Proposal Story

We got engaged while vacationing in Barcelona on a big bright sunny day in July 2017. Neil had bought the ring a month before and planned to pop the question on our Spain trip. Nervous about flying with a ring, he had it close to him the whole time fearing it would get lost or get pick pocketed. Eager to ask, he proposed on our third day in Barcelona- visiting the perfect place, the Magic Fountain. While Neil was on his knee taking photos, he took out the ring and the rest is history!
